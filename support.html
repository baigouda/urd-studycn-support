Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Open the app, go to any premium content page, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase. Your purchases are tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.

How do I cancel my VIP subscription?

To cancel your subscription: Open the Settings app on your iPhone > Tap your name at the top > Tap "Subscriptions" > Find "Study China" > Tap "Cancel Subscription." Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID. Select the purchase you want to refund and follow the instructions.

The app is not showing university data. What should I do?

Try the following steps: (1) Close and reopen the app. (2) Check your internet connection. (3) Update the app to the latest version from the App Store. (4) Delete and reinstall the app. If the issue persists, please contact us via email.

How accurate is the university match algorithm?

Our matching algorithm considers your GPA, HSK level, target degree, budget, preferred city, and major to recommend universities. The results are suggestions based on publicly available data and should be used as a starting point for your research. Always verify requirements directly with universities.

Is the scholarship information up to date?

We regularly update our scholarship database. However, scholarship details, deadlines, and requirements can change. Always verify current information on the official scholarship or university website before applying.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes, most of the app's content is available offline after the initial download. University data, guides, and tools work without an internet connection. An internet connection is only required for in-app purchases and restoring purchases.

How do I reset my profile data?

Go to Profile > Edit > modify your information and tap Save. To completely reset all data, you can delete and reinstall the app.

What does VIP include?

VIP membership gives you unlimited access to all premium content in the app, including: personalized school match reports, detailed university profiles, scholarship application guides, and major rankings. You can choose between a monthly ($9.99/month) or yearly ($79.99/year, save 33%) plan.
