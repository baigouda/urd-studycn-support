Privacy Policy

Study China - Your Guide to Studying in China

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Study China ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Profile Information: Education level, GPA, HSK level, target degree, preferred major, preferred city, and annual budget. This data is stored locally on your device only.
  • Favorites: Universities you save as favorites are stored locally on your device.
  • Checklist Data: Document checklist progress is stored locally on your device.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Purchase Data: In-app purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other personal contact information.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use cookies or web tracking technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

All profile information you enter is used solely to:

  • Generate personalized university match recommendations within the app
  • Save your preferences for a better user experience

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

4. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and AppStorage). We do not operate any servers or cloud databases. Your data remains on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security features.

5. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Apple's StoreKit for processing in-app purchases. Apple's privacy policy governs the handling of purchase transactions. We do not integrate any other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed as an educational reference tool and contains no objectionable content.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by uninstalling the app. You can also reset your profile data within the app's settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
